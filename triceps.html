Triceps Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our triceps workout routine! This workout targets your triceps muscles, helping you build strength and size in your arms.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your triceps muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of arm circles in each direction
  • 10-15 reps of triceps stretches

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your triceps:

  1. Triceps Dips: Sit on the edge of a bench with your hands placed next to your hips, fingers pointing forward. Slide your butt off the bench and lower yourself until your elbows are bent at a 90-degree angle, then push back up.
  2. Overhead Triceps Extension: Stand or sit with a dumbbell in one hand and raise it overhead. Lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending your elbow, then extend your arm to raise it back up.
  3. Triceps Pushdowns: Stand in front of a cable machine with a straight bar attached to the high pulley. Grab the bar with an overhand grip and push it downward until your arms are fully extended, then return to the starting position.
