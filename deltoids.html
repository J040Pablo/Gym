Deltoid Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our deltoid workout routine! This workout targets your deltoid muscles, helping you build strength and size in your shoulder area.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your deltoid muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of arm circles in each direction
  • 10-15 reps of shoulder stretches

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your deltoids:

  1. Front Dumbbell Raise: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold dumbbells in front of your thighs. Lift the dumbbells in front of you until your arms are parallel to the floor, then lower them back down.
  2. Lateral Dumbbell Raise: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold dumbbells by your sides. Lift the dumbbells out to the sides until your arms are parallel to the floor, then lower them back down.
  3. Rear Deltoid Fly: Sit on a bench with a slight incline and hold dumbbells in front of your thighs. Lean forward slightly and lift the dumbbells out to the sides until your arms are parallel to the floor, then lower them back down.
