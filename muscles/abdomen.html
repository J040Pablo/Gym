Abdomen Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our abdomen workout routine! This workout targets your abdominal muscles, helping you strengthen and define your core.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your abdominal muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of torso twists
  • 10-15 reps of leg swings

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your abdomen:

  1. Stomach Crunch: Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands behind your head and lift your upper body towards your knees, then lower back down.
  2. Hanging Leg Raises (Hip Flexion): Hang from a pull-up bar with your arms extended. Keep your legs straight and raise them up towards your chest, then lower them back down.
