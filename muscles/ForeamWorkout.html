Forearm Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our forearm workout routine! This workout targets your forearm muscles, helping you build strength and grip.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your forearm muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of wrist circles in each direction
  • 10-15 reps of wrist flexion and extension

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your forearms:

  1. Wrist Curls: Sit on a bench with your forearm resting on your thigh, palm facing up. Hold a dumbbell with your hand and curl your wrist upwards, then slowly lower it back down.
  2. Reverse Wrist Curls: Sit on a bench with your forearm resting on your thigh, palm facing down. Hold a dumbbell with your hand and curl your wrist upwards, then slowly lower it back down.
  3. Farmer's Walk: Hold a heavy dumbbell in each hand and walk for a certain distance or time while keeping your posture upright and shoulders back.
