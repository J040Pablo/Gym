Peito Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our peito workout routine! This workout targets your chest muscles, helping you strengthen and define your upper body.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your chest muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of arm circles
  • 10-15 reps of push-ups

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your peito:

  1. Bench Press: Lie on a flat bench with a barbell and lower it to your chest, then press it back up.
  2. Incline Dumbbell Bench Press: Lie on an incline bench with dumbbells and press them up above your chest.
  3. Cable Fly for Lower Chest (High to Low): Adjust the cable pulleys to the highest position and perform flys focusing on the lower chest.
  4. Dumbbell Fly: Lie on a flat bench with dumbbells and lower them to your sides, then bring them back up above your chest.
  5. Push-Up: Start in a plank position and lower your body towards the ground, then push back up.
