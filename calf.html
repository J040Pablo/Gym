Panturrilha Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our panturrilha workout routine! This workout targets your calf muscles, helping you strengthen and define your lower legs.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your calf muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of ankle circles
  • 10-15 reps of calf raises

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your panturrilha:

  1. Standing Calf Raise: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and lift your heels off the ground as high as possible, then lower them back down.
  2. Seated Calf Raises: Sit on a chair with your feet flat on the ground. Lift your heels as high as possible, then lower them back down.
  3. Single-Leg Calf Raise: Stand on one leg and lift your heel off the ground as high as possible, then lower it back down.
