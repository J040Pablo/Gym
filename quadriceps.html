Quadriceps Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our quadriceps workout routine! This workout targets your quadriceps muscles, helping you build strength and size in the front of your thighs.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your quadriceps. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of bodyweight squats
  • 10-15 reps of leg swings side to side

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your quadriceps:

  1. Barbell Squats: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell across your upper back. Lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back, then return to the starting position.
  2. Leg Press: Sit on a leg press machine with your back against the pad and your feet on the platform. Push the platform away by extending your knees, then return to the starting position.
  3. Walking Lunges: Stand with your feet together and hold dumbbells at your sides. Take a large step forward with one leg and lower your body until both knees are bent at a 90-degree angle, then return to the starting position and repeat with the other leg.
  4. Leg Extensions: Sit on a leg extension machine with your back against the pad and your ankles hooked under the padded lever. Extend your knees to lift the weight, then return to the starting position.
