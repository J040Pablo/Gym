Trapezius Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our trapezius workout routine! This workout targets your trapezius muscles, helping you build strength and size in your upper back and shoulders.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your trapezius muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of arm circles in each direction
  • 10-15 reps of shoulder shrugs with light dumbbells

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your trapezius:

  1. Barbell Shrugs: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell in front of your thighs. Lift your shoulders towards your ears, then lower them back down.
  2. Dumbbell Shoulder Press: Sit on a bench with back support, holding dumbbells at shoulder height with palms facing forward. Press the weights upward until your arms are fully extended, then lower them back down.
  3. Upright Rows: Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a barbell with an overhand grip. Pull the barbell straight up towards your chin, keeping it close to your body, then lower it back down.
