Biceps Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our biceps workout routine! This workout targets your biceps muscles, helping you strengthen and define your arms.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your biceps muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of arm circles
  • 10-15 reps of bicep curls with light weights

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your biceps:

  1. Dumbbell Biceps Curl: Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing forward. Curl the weights up towards your shoulders, then lower them back down.
  2. Barbell Curl: Stand with a barbell in front of you, palms facing forward. Curl the barbell up towards your shoulders, then lower it back down.
  3. Hammer Curl: Stand with a dumbbell in each hand, palms facing inward. Curl the weights up towards your shoulders, then lower them back down.
