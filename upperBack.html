Upper Back Workout

Introduction

Welcome to our upper back workout routine! This workout targets your upper back muscles, helping you build strength and improve posture.

Warm-Up

Before starting the workout, it's important to warm up your upper back muscles. Here's a simple warm-up routine:

  • 5-10 minutes of light cardio (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)
  • 10-15 reps of shoulder rolls
  • 10-15 reps of arm circles

Workout

Here are some effective exercises for your upper back:

  1. Lat Pulldowns: Sit at a lat pulldown machine and grip the bar with hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Pull the bar down towards your chest, then slowly return to the starting position.
  2. Seated Cable Rows: Sit on a rowing machine and grasp the handles with an overhand grip. Pull the handles towards your torso while keeping your back straight, then slowly return to the starting position.
  3. Face Pulls: Attach a rope handle to a cable machine at shoulder height. Grab the handles with palms facing down and pull them towards your face, keeping your elbows high and squeezing your shoulder blades together.
